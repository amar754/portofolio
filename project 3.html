Project Overview

A graduation project focused on building a tourism platform that highlights hidden and underrated destinations in Egypt, along with local events and cultural experiences. The platform organizes locations by governorates to make exploration easier and more relevant for users.

The Problem

Many unique places in Egypt are under-discovered due to :
Lack of awareness ,No centralized platform and Focus on only popular destinations.

The Goal

Help users discover hidden places in Egypt , Made exploration easier based on location and Highlight unique experiences and events

Features

  • Governorate-based navigation .
  • Dedicated page for each location .
  • Events & festivals section .
  • Clear and simple navigation .
  • Visual-focused content .

My Role

Team Leader and Designed the UI/UX Organized workflow and tasks Ensure consistency across the project and Connected and integrated all parts Contributed to front-end development

Challenges

Managing large content (many locations) ,Team coordination, Maintaining design consistency and Limited time .

Results

  • Delivered a complete tourism platform .
  • Demonstrated team leadership and organization .
  • Showcased strong UI/UX and front-end fundamentals .

Future Improvements

  • Make it fully responsive
  • Rebuild using React
  • Add APIs (maps, dynamic data)
  • Implement search & filters
  • Add user features (favorites, trip planning)
